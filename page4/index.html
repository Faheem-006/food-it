Ingredients

Instructions


For Chicken Burger


Ground chicken or chicken breast fillets


Breadcrumbs or crushed crackers for binding


Egg (optional) for binding


Onion, finely chopped


Garlic, minced


Salt and pepper for seasoning


Herbs and spices such as paprika, cumin, or parsley for flavor


Olive oil or butter for cooking


Burger buns


Lettuce leaves


Tomato slices


Cheese slices (optional)


Condiments such as mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard


If using ground chicken, place it in a mixing bowl. If using chicken breast fillets, finely chop or grind them using a food processor.

Add finely chopped onion, minced garlic, breadcrumbs or crushed crackers, salt, pepper, and any desired herbs and spices to the chicken.

Divide the mixture into equal-sized portions and shape them into patties. Make sure the patties are slightly larger than the burger buns as they will shrink slightly during cooking.

Once the skillet is hot, add the chicken patties. Cook them for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through and golden brown on the outside. Make sure the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165¬∞F (75¬∞C).

While the chicken patties are cooking, prepare the burger buns by slicing them in half.

Place lettuce leaves on the bottom half of each bun.Place a cooked chicken patty on top of the lettuce.Add tomato slices and cheese slices if desired.

Spread condiments such as mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard on the top half of the bun. Place the top half of the bun over the fillings to complete the burger.

Once assembled, serve the chicken burgers immediately while they're still warm.You can serve them with your favorite side dishes like French fries, coleslaw, or a fresh salad.

Enjoy your homemade chicken burgers!
